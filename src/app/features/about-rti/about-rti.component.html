About RTI

RTI stands for Right to Information. Right to Information Act, 2005 enables every citizen, to exercise his right, to obtain information from any Govt. Office in India. The fundamental object of the RTI Act is to bring transparency, accountability and make the nation corruption free. It also encourages every citizen in active participation in governmental activities. All you have to do is, you should file an RTI application in the Govt. Office from which you are seeking information.
